Harbinger of Snow


By MegaStarWarsFan789

Beginning life in strange new ways,
An autumn leaf you grow.
Warm and full and crisp and clear,
Harbinger of Snow.

Ripening to reds and golds,
In glittering sun you glow.
“Bring warmth,” He said, “to this cold world,
My Harbinger of Snow.”

Yet,
Your life is brief, and color fades
As all this world will show.
You fall from sky without a sigh,
O Harbinger of Snow.

Departure of Nautica


By MegaStarWarsFan789

Thunder in distance, but all here is quiet.
Heart beating harder, I know this, my mission.
Crescendo of my life, so how am I silent?
Walking through gateway, to take my position.

Sweat bead on forehead, the heavens before me.
Each mission was different, could this one mean death?
Swallow my future, for I’m in the present.
A moment all’s quiet except my own breath.

Voice in my head counting down to ignition,
Suddenly rocketfire, quaking the cockpit.
Leaving the Earth, with my own grave submission.
Away with a flash, is my fate in the rocket.

Celestia

By MegaStarWarsFan789

The young girl in all white danced around in the sun
Like a white butterfly spinning through summer grass.
She was crowned with white flowers, her dress like the snow,
And in this grassfield through the ages she passed.

Her bare feet never touched this bright world that she loved,
And she smiled and she laughed for her dear home was bright.
Still, her voice lifted higher, her song of above.
For centuries had passed yet her feet were still light.

And she danced to the harp song, bestowed by the world,
And the heavens and earth watched and smiled at her grace,
For this girl was the one who they freed from world’s pain,
So she’ll always be happy on this old land’s face.

The Evening Fog

By MegaStarWarsFan789

The misty sky is perfect white
The sun is muted of its light
Upon this single hill I stand
Alone, the last, and in God’s hand

The evening’s gone and still night comes
I think about my land and home
And in the silence of my heart
I stop the tears and with a start

I realize my time’s not come
And now I know the world’s become
A place to soothe the heartbreaks and
A place to be content again
